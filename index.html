How it works

One bot, three jobs.

ChattyBong sits in the channels your customers already use and handles the parts of support that are repetitive, time-sensitive, or easy to forget.

✦

Answers, in your voice

Trained on your docs, macros, and past tickets so responses sound like your team wrote them — not a generic bot.

◷

Remembers what's pending

Tracks open threads — a renewal, a refund, a callback — and follows up automatically instead of waiting for someone to notice.

↷

Knows when to hand off

Escalates to a human the moment a query needs judgment, with full context attached — no "can you repeat the issue?"

Case study

Ticket deflection, in practice

Order-status queries, handled before they reach a human

A mid-size e-commerce support inbox spends a large share of its day answering "where is my order" — a question the shipping API already has the answer to. ChattyBong connects to that data directly and answers instantly, in the customer's own channel.

Anything outside the script — a damaged item, a mismatched address — gets flagged and routed to a human with the order history already attached.

FAQ

Before you ask

Do you train on our data?

No. Your conversations and documents are used to answer your customers, not to train shared models. Full data handling details are shared during onboarding.

What happens when the bot doesn't know the answer?

It hands off to a human on your team with the full conversation and any relevant records attached — no cold handoff, no repeating yourself.

Are you SOC 2 or ISO certified?

Not yet — we're early-stage and honest about it. If compliance is a blocker for your team, tell us on the call and we'll walk you through our current security practices and roadmap.

Can we try it before committing?

Yes — the live demo above is a good first look, and we offer a short pilot with your own data before any contract.
